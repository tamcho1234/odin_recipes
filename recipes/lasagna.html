necessary ingredients for lasagna

  • lasagna noodles
  • ground beef
  • ricotta cheese
  • herbs

what about putting these shits together??

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Cook ground beef in a skillet until browned.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with herbs and seasonings.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.

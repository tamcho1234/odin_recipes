Maryland Crab Dip

An easy to make, one pot recipe that u just have to mix together

tasty crab dip in its full glory

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 lb jumbo lump crab meat
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Old Bay Seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup freshly shredded jack pepper
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • dash of hotsauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Add cream cheese to a mixing bowl and beat until smooth. Stir in mayo, sour cream, garlic, green onion, Old Bay seasoning, salt & pepper, Worcestershire, and lemon juice.
  3. Add both jack pepper and mozzarella cheese and hot sauce and mix until smooth. Stir in crab meat.
  4. Spread out on a baking dish, sprinkle some cheese on top, and bake for 20 min until top side is bubbling and golden
  5. Serve warm with crackers or toasted bread
know that crab dip will stay in fridge for up to 3 days, and u can rewarm in a microwave
